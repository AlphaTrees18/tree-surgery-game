Arborist Ascend: Grow, Conquer, and Sustain Your Tree Surgery Business

Welcome to Arborist Ascend! Start by doing jobs to earn money. Use your earnings to buy equipment, hire staff, and expand your tree surgery business across the UK. Focus on growth, efficiency, and sustainability as you expand your empire!

How to Play:

  • Click the "Do a Job" button to perform basic jobs and earn money.
  • Purchase equipment and hire staff to unlock higher-paying jobs and contracts.
  • Each staff member requires their own chainsaw.
  • Each crew (truck) needs a climber and a groundsman, each with their own chainsaw.
  • Unlock new cities to expand your business empire.
  • Accept contracts to earn more money, but ensure you have the required equipment and staff.
  • Monitor your fuel level and refuel when necessary.
  • Prestige to earn permanent bonuses and unlock advanced content.
